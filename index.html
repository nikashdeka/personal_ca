The Strategic Pivot: From High Cost to High Value

The Core Problem: Our current broker-led channel suffers from high commission outages (up to 16.5%), which significantly erodes profitability. A ₹1.8 Cr premium via a broker can yield less net profit than a ₹1 Cr direct sale.

The Strategic Solution: We will build a "pure commercial" direct sales channel. This pivot shifts costs from high variable commissions to manageable fixed salaries and performance incentives, fundamentally improving profitability per policy.

Key Benefits:

  • Enhanced Profitability: Drastically reduce channel costs and retain more premium.
  • Direct Customer Ownership: Build long-term relationships, enabling cross-selling and improving retention.
  • Brand Control: Communicate our value proposition directly, differentiating on expertise, not just price.

The Strategic Plan

This section details the "how" of our strategy. It breaks down our execution plan into three core pillars: a focused Go-to-Market (GTM) strategy, a world-class team development program, and a robust performance management framework to ensure sustainable growth.

We will penetrate the MSME market using a "spearhead" product to establish a foothold, then expand the client relationship to become a comprehensive risk advisor. The ideal spearhead product must be highly relevant and demonstrate clear value.
